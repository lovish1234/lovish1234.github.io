Lovish Chum

I am a MS student in Computer Science, part of the Fu Foundation School of Engineering at Columbia University, where I work on self-supervised learning of video representations. I am advised by Carl Vondrick.

From 2017 to 2019, I worked at IIIT Hyderabad, as part of CVIT where I worked with CV Jawahar and Vineeth N Balasubhramanian.

Previously, I was an undergrad at IIT Kanpur working with Aditya Nigam and Phalguni Gupta.

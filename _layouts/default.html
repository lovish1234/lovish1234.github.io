I am a Senior AI Engineer at Walgreens AI Labs where I am the part of Search and Recommendation team. Previously, I was an Machine Learning Engineer II at Amazon Web Services (AWS) - AI where I was the part of Rekognition team. I aim to create buisness impact through my scientific and engineering skills in computer vision and language models (especially LLMs).

Earlier I was a Research Asosicate at Dept. of Electrical Engineering, Columbia University with Prof. Shih-Fu Chang. I received MS(by research/thesis track) in Computer Science from Fu Foundation School of Engineering at Columbia University, where I worked on future scene graph prediction with Carl Vondrick.

From 2017 to 2019, I worked at CVIT where I worked with CV Jawahar and Vineeth N Balasubhramanian. Even earlier, I was an undergrad at IIT Kanpur.

I am always looking for opportunities. Welcome to contact via e-mail if you think it can be a good fit.
